History
D'hoTel is a legendary hotel in Jakarta open
since 1999 providing impeccable service for its
guests. The hotel has undergone renovations
and new building construction in 2003, 2005
and 2009.
In 2003, D'hoTel built a bigger and newer hotel
building, renovating the old building. In 2005,
D'HoTel added facilities such as a spa,
swimming pool and gym.
Previously, D'HoTel only provided 2 types of
rooms, 1 bed room and 2 bed rooms. Until 2009
D'HoTel provided a new room type, namely the
luxurious suite, which contained 3 bedrooms
and a kitchen.
